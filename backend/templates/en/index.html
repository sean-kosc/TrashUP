Welcome to TrashUP

TrashUP is a smart platform for monitoring public trash bins, designed to prevent illegal dumping and improve urban waste management.

  • View and annotate the status of bins (full/empty) from photos.
  • Check statistics and an interactive map for efficient monitoring.
  • Be part of a Green IT and citizen initiative.

This project was carried out as part of the Master Green IT.
